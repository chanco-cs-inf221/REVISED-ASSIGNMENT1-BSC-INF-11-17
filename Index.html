PROJECT TWO

MARTENAL DEARTH

DISCRIPTION
The aim of this project is to reduce deaths that occurs among women and infants. It has been discovered that a lot of women who are about to give birth die on their way to hospitals due to long distance to the hospitals. This project has built many clinics to reduce the distance also for them to reach at the clinics faster and be able to deliver in time.

PROGRESS

The project has successed a lot! it has helped a lot of women.
